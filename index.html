About Me


Hello! My name is Aishah Asli, and I’m currently pursuing a diploma in Business Management at UiTM Kota Kinabalu, Sabah. As a first-semester student, I’m embracing this exciting new chapter of my academic journey while exploring my passion for entrepreneurship and management. One of my biggest dreams is to open my own restaurant in the future, a vision inspired by my love for cooking and my desire to fulfill my father’s long-cherished aspiration.

Cooking is not just a hobby for me, it’s something that brings me immense joy and allows me to express myself creatively. I love experimenting with new recipes and flavors, imagining how they could one day become part of the menu at my dream restaurant.

When I’m not busy with studies, I enjoy spending time with my diploma friends. We’ve created countless memories together, from studying for exams to hanging out and exploring new places. Their companionship and support have made my university life even more fulfilling. Although I may come across as an introvert initially, those who know me well understand that I have a fun, crazy side that often surprises people. I value meaningful connections and cherish the time I spend with my family and friends.

Being the eldest among my siblings has taught me responsibility and shaped my determination to achieve my goals. I strive to work hard and make my dreams a reality while honoring the values and aspirations of my family. Life for me is a journey of growth, exploration, and working towards building a bright and fulfilling future.
